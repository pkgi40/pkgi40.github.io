Home

This tutorial collection guides users from understanding Papyrus4Manufacturing to applying it in complex use cases. All of the use cases are implemented and tested in LocalSEA, the Industry 4.0 & 5.0 testbed under the management of DRT/LIST/DILS.

Tutorials

  • 01: Led blink

Monitor and Control a led to blink using an AAS DT of led.

  • 02: T3WP moving

Control a the TurtleBot3 Waffle Pi robot to move forward/backward with.

  • 03: UR3e control

Control a UR3e robot for basic Pick and Place tasks.

  • 04: Multiple protocols

Use +10 network protocols to change color of an object.

  • 05: AAS-OPC UA Information models conversion

Convert an AAS information model into OPC UA information model.

LocalSEA

LocalSEA is a small-scale and didatic testbed under the management of DRT/LIST/DILS. The tests primarily focus on the software components and technologies developed by the department. It is equipped with compact industrial equipment, IoT devices, and robots.
