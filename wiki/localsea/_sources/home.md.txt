# Home  

This tutorial collection guides users from understanding Papyrus4Manufacturing 
to applying it in complex use cases. All of the use cases are implemented 
and tested in LocalSEA, the Industry 4.0 & 5.0 testbed under the management 
of DRT/LIST/DILS. 

---

### Tutorials

- [01: Led blink](ledblink.md)  
> Monitor and Control a led to blink using an AAS DT of led.       

- [02: T3WP moving](t3wpmove.md)  
> Control a the TurtleBot3 Waffle Pi robot to move forward/backward with.  

- [03: UR3e control](ur3econtrol.md)  
> Control a UR3e robot for basic Pick and Place tasks.   

- [04: Multiple protocols](mprotocols.md)  
> Use +10 network protocols to change color of an object.  

- [05: AAS-OPC UA Information models conversion](aas2ua.md)  
> Convert an AAS information model into OPC UA information model.        

---

### LocalSEA  

LocalSEA is a small-scale and didatic testbed under the management of 
DRT/LIST/DILS. The tests primarily focus on the software components and 
technologies developed by the department. 
It is equipped with compact industrial equipment, IoT devices, and robots.
