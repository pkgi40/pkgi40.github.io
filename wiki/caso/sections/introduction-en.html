Introduction back to ToC

CASO (Context-Aware System Observation) is an ontology developed in 2019 by TSCF, Irstea Clermont-Fd. From 2020, Irstea becomes INRAE after a fusion with INRA, then CASO is now under the maintain of UR1471 TSCF - Research Unit Technologies and Information Systems for Agrosystems, INRAE. This ontology provides classes and properties that enable users to model the context-aware system and its observations in a semantic method. This online documentation is the specification of the CASO version 1.2. The lastest update is in January 2020.
